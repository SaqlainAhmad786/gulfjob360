Privacy Policy

At GulfJob360, we collect the personal information you provide during registration to help you find better job opportunities. Some of this information may be shared with employers to assist them in selecting the right candidates for their openings.

Personal Information refers to any details that can identify an individual, including but not limited to, your first and last name, physical address, email address, or other contact information. Generally, you can browse the GulfJob360 website without disclosing your identity or sharing any personal information.

Cookies and Other Tracking Technologies

Some pages on GulfJob360 use cookies and other tracking technologies to enhance your browsing experience. A "cookie" is a small text file that collects information about website activity, such as remembering your preferences or previously entered personal information.

Most browsers let you manage cookies, including accepting, blocking, or deleting them. You can configure your browser to notify you when cookies are received or to block them entirely. However, if you block or delete cookies, you may need to re-enter your user ID and password to access certain parts of the website.

Tracking technologies may collect details such as your IP address, domain and host names, browser type, operating system, click patterns, and the dates and times you access our site. These tools help us improve the website and enhance your user experience. Additionally, we may analyze non-personal information for trends and statistical purposes.

Account Protection

Your password is the key to your GulfJob360 account, and you are solely responsible for all activities conducted under your username. It is crucial to keep your password secure and confidential.

Do not share your password with anyone. If you choose to disclose your password or personal information, you will be fully accountable for any actions taken under your account. This may result in a loss of control over your personal information and could lead to legally binding actions carried out on your behalf.

If you suspect that your password has been compromised, change it immediately to protect your account and personal data.

Security

At GulfJob360, the security of your personal information is a top priority. We treat your data as a valuable asset and use various security measures, including encryption, password protection, and physical safeguards, to protect it from unauthorized access and disclosure. However, no method of online transmission or electronic storage is completely secure. While we employ industry-standard practices to safeguard your personal information, we cannot guarantee absolute security. There is always a risk that third parties may unlawfully intercept transmissions or that other users may misuse the information you provide. Therefore, although we are committed to protecting your data, we cannot guarantee that your personal information or communications will always remain private, and you should remain cautious when sharing sensitive information online.

Usage

By providing GulfJob360 with your personal information, you consent to its use, transfer, processing, and storage on our secure servers. We will not share your information with third parties for marketing or related activities without your prior consent. However, this does not include our group companies, holding or subsidiary companies, and service partners or associates, who may access your information for operational purposes. Please note that if you choose to make your profile visible, all details associated with your profile will be accessible to third parties.
